───────────────────────────────────
Universal clipboard                
───────────────────────────────────

Usually on Linux, you need Ctrl +
Shift + C/V in terminal and Ctrl +
C/V elsewhere. These work
everywhere.

S-C          Copy                  
S-X          Cut (not in terminal) 
S-V          Paste                 
S-C-V        Clipboard manager
